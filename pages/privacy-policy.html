Privacy Policy for Borders Bike King

Borders Bike King ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal data is handled securely and in accordance with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you visit our website, contact us, purchase products, book services, or rent bicycles.

1. Who We Are

Borders Bike King
Email: info@bikekingborders.co.uk
Website: https://www.bikekingborders.com

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy or your personal information, please contact us using the details above.

2. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Billing address
  • Delivery address
  • Order details
  • Bicycle service booking information
  • Rental booking information
  • Enquiry messages submitted through contact forms

Information Collected Automatically

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Device information
  • Pages visited
  • Website usage data
  • Referring websites
  • Session information

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Process bike and accessory orders
  • Deliver products
  • Manage bicycle servicing bookings
  • Manage bike rental reservations
  • Respond to enquiries
  • Provide customer support
  • Improve website functionality
  • Detect and prevent fraud
  • Meet legal and accounting obligations
  • Send service updates and order confirmations

Where you have provided consent, we may also send promotional offers and marketing communications.

4. Legal Basis for Processing

Contract

To fulfil purchases, rentals, repairs, servicing, and other services requested by you.

Legitimate Interests

  • Improving our website and services
  • Business administration
  • Customer support
  • Website security

Consent

  • Marketing emails
  • Newsletter subscriptions
  • Non-essential cookies

Legal Obligation

To comply with tax, accounting, and consumer protection laws.

5. Online Orders and Payments

Payments may be processed securely through third-party payment providers. We do not store full payment card details on our own systems.

6. Bicycle Servicing and Rental Records

To provide servicing and rental services, we may store:

  • Bicycle model information
  • Service history
  • Repair records
  • Rental booking history
  • Customer contact details

7. Cookies

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Maintain website functionality
  • Improve user experience
  • Analyse website traffic
  • Remember preferences

You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

8. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell personal information.

We may share information with:

  • Delivery and courier providers
  • Payment processors
  • Website hosting providers
  • IT and technical service providers
  • Professional advisers
  • Law enforcement or regulatory authorities when legally required

9. Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to complete transactions, provide customer support, maintain servicing records, meet legal obligations, and resolve disputes.

10. Data Security

  • SSL encryption
  • Secure website hosting
  • Restricted access controls
  • Regular software updates
  • Secure password practices

11. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Restrict processing
  • Object to certain processing activities
  • Request data portability
  • Withdraw consent at any time

12. Marketing Communications

You can unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link included in our communications.

13. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to external websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of third-party websites.

14. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed toward children under 13 years of age.

15. Complaints

If you are dissatisfied with how we handle your personal information, please contact us first.

You may also lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO):
https://ico.org.uk/

16. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page together with an updated revision date.
